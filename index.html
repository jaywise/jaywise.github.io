Project mock-up/proposal:



For my project, I am aiming to create an interactive dashboard. The audience will be students accessing the PAPO app, whose purpose is to empower English language learners in Brazil. The dashboard will consist of sliders and radio buttons that in turn morph graphic representations of data in "real time". For example, a user may be asked, "What is your goal for learning English?" The user will select the reasons and the "weight" of each, e.g., 40% social, 25% professional advancement, 35% entertainment.

After each iteration, or learning cycle, with the app, the user will be asked to reflect upon the previously declard learning goals. To extend the above example, users will be prompted to tweak the proportionality of reasons for learning English based upon the learning cycle and their new "station" in life and in the English learning process.

Finally, the dashboard will be responsive to data collected during the learning cycle of the app, utilizing patterns in user behavior to suggest ways in which learning goals may have changed. Within this culminatig, metacognitive phase of each learning cycle, the app will display a tooltip or other light visual intervention when users navigate to each graph. The suggestion may sound something like, "We noticed you select learning models that are more entertainment-minded. Is it possible that your learning goals have changed here? Take a minute to look at your previous goals and tweak them according to how your perception of your learning process has changed.
